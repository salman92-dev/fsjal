FSJAL, pronounced as "fuh-sjal," is an intentionally misspelled version of the word "visual." It's a humorous distortion of the English language, which is a common theme in the world of internet memes. FSJAL is closely associated with crudely drawn, intentionally low-quality images and often includes poorly written captions, adding to its charm.

FSJAL is an internet meme that originated from a crudely drawn image of a rage face character. The meme became popular on imageboards and forums in the early 2010s, particularly on 4chan.
